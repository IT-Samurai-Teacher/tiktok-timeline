The TikTok Revolution

ByteDance's Rise and America's Response

2012
ByteDance Founded

Founded by Zhang Yiming in Beijing. ByteDance started as a news aggregator company, showing early signs of powerful AI-driven content recommendation systems.

2024
Pre-Ban Developments

TikTok's dominant position raises concerns with over 170 million active users in the US alone. Growing global debate over social media regulation and data sovereignty.

January 19, 2025
U.S. Ban Implementation

TikTok is removed from major app stores. Service disruption affects millions of US users as the government cites national security concerns.

January 2025
Supreme Court Decision

Court upholds legislation mandating ByteDance divestment, setting a legal precedent for foreign-owned app regulation.

January 19, 2025
Temporary Service Restoration

President-elect proposes 50% US ownership solution, potentially creating a new model for international tech governance.
